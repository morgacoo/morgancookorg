Once upon a time...

If you were hiring me, I'd be representing you in court (usually not the best time to meet a person). But today, I've traded in my legal treatises for web design tutorials, my oral arguments for hashtags, and the U.S. Penal Code for computer code. Gone are clients coming to me with legal problems, and in their place are clients looking to grow their businesses and build their brands - and I couldn't be happier.

I focus my work on front-end design utilizing HTML, CSS, bootstrap, jQuery, and Javascript to bring a clean, functional, and fun products to my clients. From the initial concept stage through site launch, I work with my clients to ensure they receive a final product that suits their needs and reflects their personality. I hope you like it.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
